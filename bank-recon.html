Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing Altametrics Automated Bank Reconciliation.

How secure are the bank feeds?

We utilize industry-standard, read-only Plaid/Yodlee financial integrations with enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption. We never have the ability to move funds.

How does it handle cash drop variances?

If the POS says a $500 drop was made, but the bank counted $498, the system will highlight the exception for the accountant to manually book to the 'Cash Over/Short' GL account.

Can it reconcile safe balances?

Yes! It integrates with smart-safes (like Brink's or Loomis) to track exactly how much change is in the store vs in transit vs at the bank.
